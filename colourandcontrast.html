Colours illustrated

There are several ways to specify colours on the web. By name:

  • black black
  • grey grey
  • pink pink
  • lightgrey lightgrey
  • white white
The way colours are specified on the web is by supplying numbers for red, green and blue from 0-255. These are expressed as hex digits 0123456789ABCDEF. If the r,g,b match, they're a grey.
